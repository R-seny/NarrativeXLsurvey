Instructions

Welcome and thank you for agreeing to participate in our research!

This study is about reading book snippets and evaluating associated summaries.

Each question will feature a book snippet and two associated summaries, one accurate and one inaccurate. You need to indicate which summary is the correct one.

*Note that book snippets were selected randomly, so they often start and end abruptly (for example, in the middle of a word). It is also (understandably) confusing to start reading a book in a middle of a narrative. Please try to use your best judgement to compensate for these difficulties.
